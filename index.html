The Virtuoso

Oduwa, the Nigerian-born Afrobeat virtuoso, commands attention with a style that exudes opulence and artistry. From humble beginnings at age 11, creating custom ringtones on his uncle’s phone, Oduwa's journey has been one of discovery and mastery.

His sound, influenced by icons like Akon, P-Square, The Cavemen, SDC, Burna Boy, and Wizkid, blends the timeless rhythms of Afrobeat with sleek, contemporary vibrations—creating a soundscape that is as luxurious as it is unforgettable.

With the release of his single "SAPA" on May 7, 2024, Oduwa redefined Afrobeat, imbuing his music with playful sophistication and engaging energy. "SAPA" reflects not only his mastery of the genre but his ability to craft experiences that are both entertaining and deeply connective.
